MARCELA MONTOYA ARTIST

ABOUT ME

"The purpose of art is washing the dust of daily life off of our souls" Pablo Picasso.

Marcela Montoya




My name is Marcela. I was born in 1997 in Honduras. In 2014 I moved to the UK to join my family here. Since then, I demonstrated a lot of interest in art. I produced a lot of work in secondary school and got my GCSE in art with A. I decided then to continue developing my knowledge and techniques to produce art. I was accepted at Coleg Ceredigion in 2015 to continue with my dream. I graduated in BTEC Level 3 Extended Diploma in Art and Design after two Years. Currently I am in my second Year for BA Fine Arts at Aberystwyth University. Nothing in this life makes more happy than doing my work. I am creating more and better. I am actualli gaining skills in painting, printmaking, drawing, photography, book illustration, experimental film, installation and site-specific performance, this definitelly allows me to connect traditional skills with contemporary practice and theory. The School of Art in Aberystwyth is 2nd in the UK for teaching experience and 3rd in the UK for teaching quality.
